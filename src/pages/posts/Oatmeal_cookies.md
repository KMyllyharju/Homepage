## Actual recipe

<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>400 g flour</li>
<li>400 g sugar</li>
<li>400 g rolled oats</li>
<li>3/4 tsp baking powder</li>
<li>3/4 tsp salt</li>
<li>1/2 tsp MSG</li>
<li>1 tsp vanilla sugar</li>
<li>200 g melted butter</li>
<li>150 ml milk</li>
<li>90 ml rice syrup / light syrup</li>
</ul>

<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Gather and combine dry ingredients</li>
<li>Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter, so when scooped onto a surface it gradually flattens and spreads but is firm enough to retain a general shape.</li>
<li>Bake at 190°C (or 170°C for a convection oven) for 11-14 mins, until the surface turns golden brown.</li>
</ol>
